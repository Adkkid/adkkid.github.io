The Adirondack Arc Memory game


Everyone has natural human rights. This game was created by the Adirondack Arc Rights Work group to teach you about what rights you have.


To play this game, tap or click on a card. Then, try to find the card with the same picture to match the two together. When you match all of the cards, you win! Be sure to watch all of the videos every time you match a card.
